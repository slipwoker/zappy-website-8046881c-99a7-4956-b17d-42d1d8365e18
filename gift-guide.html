Gift questions, gently answered.

From choosing the perfect piece to timing and presentation, find thoughtful answers for every gift-giving moment.

01

How do I choose a piece if I don't know their exact style?

Start with timeless silhouettes — a delicate chain, classic studs, or a simple bangle. Our collection is curated to feel personal yet universally loved. Look at what they already wear: gold or silver, minimal or statement. When in doubt, choose a piece that tells a story, and we'll include a handwritten note to share it.

02

What's the best timing for ordering a gift?

Most of our jewelry is crafted to order with a lead time of 5–10 business days. We recommend ordering at least two weeks before your occasion to allow for personalization and unhurried shipping. For last-minute gifts, explore our Ready to Ship collection, which arrives beautifully packaged within 3–5 days.

03

How does the presentation and packaging work?

Every piece arrives in our signature soft-touch box with a suede interior, wrapped in tissue and sealed with a wax stamp. The unboxing is designed to feel like a quiet ceremony — no logos screaming, just a gentle reveal. Gift wrapping and a personal message card are always complimentary.

04

Can I personalize a piece with engraving?

Yes — many of our pendants, cuffs, and signet rings can be engraved with initials, a date, or a short phrase. Each engraving is done by hand in our studio. Add your personalization at checkout, and we'll send a digital proof within 48 hours for your approval before we begin.

05

What if the recipient wants to exchange or return the gift?

We want every gift to feel right. Our extended holiday return window gives recipients 60 days to exchange or return unworn pieces. We'll cover return shipping and help them find the perfect alternative — or issue a full refund, no questions asked. Gift receipts are always included.

06

How can I request personalized guidance?

We offer complimentary one-on-one virtual styling sessions. Share a few details about the recipient — their style, the occasion, your budget — and our curator will hand-select three pieces and walk you through each one over a 20-minute video call. It's the easiest way to gift with confidence.
